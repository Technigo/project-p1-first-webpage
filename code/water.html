Letting the Mind Flow Like Water

When I sit in my favorite place by a river, I can calm down and enjoy nature and its beauty. Flowing water always reminds me how quickly a moment - time - passes. It reminds me how important it is to appreciate the moment and to perceive it consciously.

Remeber: You must not take anything for granted. You should be grateful. You should spend your time wisely and spend it with the people who are good for you.
