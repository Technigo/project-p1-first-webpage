It's Brunch O'clock

The best Sundays start with a good brunch in a cozy place, surrounded by friends and a large selection of delicious food. I think brunch is always possible - whether in summer on a beautiful terrace in the shade of trees or a sunshade, whether in autumn when it is wet and uncomfortable outside, or whether in winter to recover from the stress of Christmas followed by a walk in the snow.
