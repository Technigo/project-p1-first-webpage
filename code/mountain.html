Nature-Bound

Up up to the top. Breathing the fresh air and feeling so close to nature is one of the best experiences. I love to spend my time outside - alone or with friends. Often nature helps me to find myself again. Hiking in the mountains is connected with effort, you feel your body better than ever, get to know your limits better and learn to rely on your strength.

I had the most beautiful and lasting experiences with my friends in the mountains. Spending time outdoors and storming a summit together is a great bonding experience. We prefer to stay overnight in the huts of the Swiss Aplenclub (SAC)
