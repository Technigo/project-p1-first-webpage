Take a Deep Breath

Pilates is one of my youngest loves. It gives me the opportunity to feel myself and ground myself. When I practice Pilates, I consciously take time to take care only of myself and do something good for myself. I treat my body to something like a reset and supply it with new energy. In addition, I usually manage to leave my everyday worries behind, to pacify my thoughts from fears and to come to rest.
