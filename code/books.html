Getting Lost in a Book

Finding time to read a good book gives me the opportunity to leave the daily, sometimes busy life behind and dive into another world. I sometimes look forward to reading my book all day long after work.

What I like best are books that take place in a country or city I already know. This gives me the opportunity to return to this place and to get to know it through the eyes of another person - often in a different time period.
