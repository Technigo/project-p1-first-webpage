Coffee Lover

A cup of coffee is like a warm hug from a friend and always reminds me how much emotion such a small cup of warm liquid can evoke. How a coffee tastes always depends on who I drink it with, where I drink it and to what circumstances. But one thing is always the case: A coffee means a small reward for me.
