Amy Brown: Medieval Literatures, Modern Cultures

Researcher in Medieval Literatures and Contemporary Reception

I'm an early career researcher with a wide range of interests and teaching experience. I've worked on both medieval English and medieval French, and from there my interests in translation and adaptation have joined my interests in gender and sexuality to bring me into the field of contemporary adaptation and reception.
